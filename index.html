Our Story

Serving authentic vegetarian food with warmth and tradition for more than five decades.

Since 1972

Sharma Vaishno Dhaba has been serving hot, wholesome and home-style vegetarian food since 1972.

What began as a small roadside stop has become a trusted landmark for travelers visiting Katra and Mata Vaishno Devi.

Known for Rajma Chawal, Paneer Pakoda, Tandoori Parathas and traditional North Indian meals, we continue to serve every guest with the same warmth and hospitality that started over 50 years ago.
