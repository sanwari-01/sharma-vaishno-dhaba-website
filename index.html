Our Story

For over 50 years, Sharma Vaishno Dhaba has been serving hot, wholesome, home-style vegetarian food to travelers and families passing through Chhurta, Domel, Jammu & Kashmir.

What began as a simple roadside dhaba in 1972 has grown into a beloved landmark trusted by locals, tourists, and travelers.

  • Paneer Pakoda
  • Tandoori Paratha
  • Traditional North Indian Meals
  • Tea & Refreshments
